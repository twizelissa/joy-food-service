[GINMITEI] New seasonal menu launching next week!

Hello! We are Joy Food Service, a restaurant company based in Fukushima Prefecture. Today, we will introduce our latest seasonal menu at GINMITEI.

  2. Exclusive Weekend Dinner Options
  3. New sake selection featuring local breweries
  4. Reservation information

1. Autumn Special - Matsutake Mushroom Course

Our autumn special features the prized matsutake mushroom in various preparations. The course includes matsutake soup, grilled matsutake with premium wagyu beef, and matsutake rice. This limited-time offering showcases the distinctive aroma and flavor that makes matsutake so celebrated in Japanese cuisine.

2. Exclusive Weekend Dinner Options

Starting next week, we will be offering exclusive dinner course options on weekends. These special courses will feature the freshest seasonal ingredients selected by our chef each morning. Limited to just 10 reservations per night, this intimate dining experience highlights the best of our kitchen's capabilities.

3. New sake selection featuring local breweries

We've partnered with three local sake breweries to offer a curated selection of regional sake that pairs perfectly with our autumn menu. Guests can enjoy a sake flight of three varieties, complete with tasting notes and information about each brewery.

4. Reservation information

Due to the limited availability of our seasonal ingredients, we strongly recommend making reservations at least three days in advance. Reservations can be made by phone or through our website. For groups larger than 6 people, please contact us directly to arrange accommodations.

5. Special promotions for our loyal customers

Members of our loyalty program will receive a complimentary dessert with their meal during the first two weeks of our new menu launch. Not a member yet? Sign up during your visit to start enjoying exclusive benefits and promotions.
